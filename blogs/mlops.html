FinOps: Taking Control of Your Cloud Spending

FinOps · Multi-cloud cost management · Cost visibility and monitoring · CloudHealth

This post describes engineering lessons from designing and validating a production-grade CI/CD pipeline, focusing on automation, testing, and real-world readiness.

Overview

In real systems, a pipeline must be predictable, observable, and resilient. Basic tutorials don’t cover rollback strategies or production-level test harnesses.

Architecture

The pipeline uses:

  • Terraform for infrastructure provisioning
  • GitHub Actions for multi-stage CI/CD
  • Automatic health checks and rollbacks

Testing & Validation

Load tests and dummy traffic were used to evaluate behaviour under stress. Observability signals helped refine alerting and failure handling.

Key Takeaways

Production readiness means anticipating failure, validating rollback paths, and automating observability — not just passing a green checkmark.
